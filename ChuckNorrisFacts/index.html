Chuck Norris knows where you are...

Carlos Ray "Chuck" Norris (born March 10, 1940) is an American martial artist and actor. After serving in the United States Air Force, he began his rise to fame as a martial artist, and has since founded his own school, Chun Kuk Do.

Norris appeared in a number of action films, such as Way of the Dragon, in which he starred alongside Bruce Lee. He played the starring role in the television series Walker, Texas Ranger from 1993 until 2001.

His net worth ,as of 2013, is around $70,000,000.
